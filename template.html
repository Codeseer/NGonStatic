About Us

N-Gon Games was founded in Atlanta, Georgia in 2011 by Tim Frank, Connor McCarthy, and Ajani Thomas. Born from a love for and a desire to create great games, the company has several games in development and scheduled for release this year. N-Gon Games currently works with 9 game developers, including programmers, artists, sound designers, level designers, and scriptwriters.
